Wind Speed

This graph does not show that there's much conclusive evidence that wind speed is affected by latitude. But, it also looks like there are slightly higher wind speeds north of the equator, though the number of data points we have here isn't especially large. We also have much more density in our northern data points, which can be traced to the cities polled as part of the data gathering efforts.
